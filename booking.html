Instructions for Booking a Music Club Room for Rehearsals

 

Choose a Time:

- Determine your preferred date and time for the rehearsal. Consider selecting a few alternative options in case your first choice is not available.

 

Fill Out the Request:

- Select the desired room from the available options.
- Enter the date and time of the rehearsal.
- Provide details about the group or performer (name, number of participants, contact information).
- Add any additional comments or special requirements if necessary.

Arrival at the Club:

- Arrive at the club 10-15 minutes before your rehearsal time.
- Meet with the administrator or follow the instructions provided for accessing the room.

 

After the Rehearsal:

- Ensure the room is left in good condition.
- Return all equipment to its proper place and turn off any devices.
